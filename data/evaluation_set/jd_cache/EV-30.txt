As the Product Manager of this group, you are the product leader for growth initiatives and new product launches for consumer facing experiences within the Payments and Subscriptions team. You Will: Lead the long term strategy for subscriptions including but not limited to growing existing subscriber base, increasing retention, international growth and developer led growth. Act as the lead for consumer facing experiences for all real currency transactions on Roblox. Lead the end to end strategy and execution for increasing developer contributions to subscriptions and future offerings. You Have: 3-6 years of product management experience, with expertise in the consumer product offerings A consistent track record of aligning teams and prioritizing features/resources to deliver an excellent product in monetization, payments, subscriptions or related fintech space High-reaching drive: Worked in a fast paced environment, with bold priorities, through the product life cycle from planning, development, through to launch. Strong opinions and a willingness to make calls with limited information. A high degree of self-direction and a get stuff done demeanor. Technical approach: delving into the product, platform and implementation details. Ability to engage with engineering to design scalable and extensible solutions. Data-driven approach: Proficiency identifying, defining, tracking and acting upon metrics. Familiarity with experimentation and A/B testing methods. Experience identifying critical metrics and working with engineering and analytics on instrumentation and monitoring. Strong written and verbal communication skills: Ability to convey strategies, priorities, and tradeoffs at the 30K foot level, as well as produce clear and detailed product and technical specifications If you are an entrepreneurial product leader, who doesn’t hesitate to propose high-reaching ideas and put them into action, you’ll be a great fit.
